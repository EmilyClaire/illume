Breaking Stereotypes

We strive tp challenge other people's perceptions of race. By implementing an interface to chat with others, we hope to achieve our goal.

We encourage a friendly and open space to speak your mind and express your feelings about a given topic. After chatting for 5 minutes, you will be prompted to choose what ethnicity you think the person you chatted with is.
